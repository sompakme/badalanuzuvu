Invest in the Right Supportive Bra

Gilbert Carrasquillo//Getty Images

Pugh says buying and investing in good bras is also a must. "Nothing ruins an outfit more than the wrong undergarments. Take the time to get professionally fitted—it makes a huge difference. Splurge on a plunge bra, an everyday T-shirt bra, and a good strapless option, too."

When in Doubt, Go Oversized

Jacopo Raule//Getty Images

Pugh invites you to shop in the men's section or go up a size for a more relaxed look. "When you don’t want your bust to be the focal point of your look, buy your tops a size or two larger than you normally would. This gives you a more relaxed and casual look that adds comfort! If things are too boxy in the waist for your liking, have your tailor take it in, leaving the extra room in the bust, or pair it with a high-waisted pant or skirt to bring more shape."
